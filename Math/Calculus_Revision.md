# Integration

## Definite Integrals

### Primitive method

$$
\int_a^bf(x)\,dx = F(b)-F(a)\,; \frac{d}{dx}F(x) = f(x)
$$

**_Note:_** $F(x)$ is called the **antiderivative** or **primitive** of $f(x)$;

### Sum of Riemann method

$$
\int_a^bf(x)\,dx = \lim_{n \to \infty}\sum_{i=1}^n\left(\frac{b-a}{n}\cdot f(x_i)\right)
$$
